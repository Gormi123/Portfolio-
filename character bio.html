Sivagami
Movie:bahubali

Raja Mata, played by Ramya Krishna is the foster mother of our main hero bahubali, Raja Mata is a powerful character as she is the one who takes care of the mahishmati kingdom. She is a skilled fighter, feared by her enemies. She is a true leader who treats everyone equally. She is the one who takes care of bahubali and ballala deva and she teaches them to be worthy heirs of the throne and when the time comes she decides to make one of them a king of Mahishmathi. She won the hearts of fans with her superb character and acting skills and she is one of those characters who will be very close to our hearts.
